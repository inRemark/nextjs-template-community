## Getting Started Guide

Welcome to our platform! This guide will help you get started quickly.

## Step 1: Create an Account

Visit the registration page and fill in basic information:

- Email address
- Set a secure password
- Verify your email

### Account Verification

After registration, you will receive a verification email. Please click the link in the email to complete verification.

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After logging in, complete your profile:

- Upload an avatar
- Fill in basic information
- Set preference options

## Step 3: Explore Features

Familiarize yourself with the main features:

### Console

- View data statistics
- Manage projects
- Set permissions

### Article Management

- Create new articles
- Edit existing content
- Publish and share

## Step 4: Get Help

If you encounter problems:

- Check help documentation
- Watch video tutorials
- Contact customer support

## FAQ

**Q: How do I change my password?**

A: Go to "Personal Settings" > "Security Settings" > "Change Password".

**Q: What if I forget my password?**

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset via email.

## Next Steps

Congratulations! You've completed the basic setup. Now you can:

- Create your first project
- Invite team members
- Explore advanced features
